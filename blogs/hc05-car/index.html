Introduction to HC-05 and making car

What is HC-05?

HC-05 module is an easy to use Bluetooth SPP (Serial Port Protocol) Slave module, for wireless serial connection setup. Serial port Bluetooth module is fully qualified Bluetooth V2.0+EDR (Enhanced Data Rate) 3Mbps Modulation with 2.4GHz radio transceiver and baseband .
It can be simply used in blace of normal serial used in arduino by default , just via bluetooth.

Making a HC-05 bluetooth mobile controlled car



materials- any Motor driver(i used L293D MOTOR DRIVER IC) , Chases ,HC-05 bluetooth module , jumper wires , adapter/battery , 2 wheels and 2 motors for it , Arduino uno/mega/mini/micro , android mobile with bluetooth support , Bluetooth Terminal App in it. Download Now On android

Lets Begin Making It-

First Make the following cicuit from hc-05 to arduino

connect-
vcc -> 3v
gnd(hc-05) -> gnd(arduino)
tx -> rx
rx -> tx


tap the image to enlarge



now connect motor pins via motor driver to arduino.

1.Connect motors to motor drivers
2.Connect left motor positive to pin 3 and negative to pin 4
3.Connect right motor positive to pin 4 and negative to pin 5

If motors rotate opposite , you may flip positive and negative or left motor to right motor.
Join the motors tho Chases.
